ingredients:
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thigh
- 6 Tbsp EVOO
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp allspice
- 3/4 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1 pinch cayenne
- salt
- pepper
directions:
- Slice the chicken into large chunks, approx 5-6 pieces per breast or 3-4 per thigh
- Whisk 4 Tbsp olive oil, spices, 3/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper. Add chicken
- Marinate chicken at least 1 hour or max overnight
- Bake at 400F on oiled baking sheet for 15 minutes or until cooked through. Turn the chicken halfway through cooking
- Slice the chicken into thin strips once cooled a bit
- Saute chicken in 2 Tbsp oil in skillet over medium heat
