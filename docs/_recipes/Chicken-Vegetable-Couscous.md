yield: 4
time: 0.75
tags:
- american
ingredients:
- 2 sweet potatoes
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 Tbsp honey or maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 lb boneless, skinless, chicken thigh
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1.75 cup low sodium chicken broth
- 1 cup pearl couscous
- 1 head broccoli, cut into small florets
- 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- salt
- pepper
directions:
- Preheat oven to 350F
- Cube sweet potatoes, place onto parchment lined baking sheet. Drizzle with 1 Tbsp oil and honey, sprinkle 1/2 tsp chili powder, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp cayenne, salt, and pepper
- Bake for 20-30 minutes until tender, flipping halfway through
- Cook chicken in 1 Tbsp oil over medium-high heat. After adding chicken to pan, immediately sprinkle with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Cook until done, about 5-8 minutes per side
- Add broth and couscous to large pot or dutch oven, then set broccoli florets on top. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover to cook for 8-10 minutes. After 5 minutes, give a stir
- Dice cooked chicken and add to pot along with cheddar cheese, stir to combine. Add roasted sweet potato and fold in
