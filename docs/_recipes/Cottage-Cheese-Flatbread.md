---
layout: recipe
yield: 4
time: 0.25
tags:
- bread
ingredients:
- 1 cup AP flour
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp baking powder
- 3/4 cup whipped cottage cheese
directions:
- Mix flour, salt, and baking powder until well combined
- Mix in cottage cheese until a thick dough is formed. Can add extra flour or a little water if needed
- Knead by hand until smooth
- Divide into four balls, flatten to 1/4 inch thickness
- Cook in skillet over medium heat, 2-3 minutes per side

- []
- []

- []
- []
notes:
- Its fine with regular and not whipped cottage cheese
- Store in fridge, or freeze for up to two months
