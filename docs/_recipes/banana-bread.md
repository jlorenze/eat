ingredients:
- 4 bananas
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- pinch salt
- 1/4 cup nuts (we like pecans and a lot of extra text to see what happens)

directions:
- Beat the eggs, then cream with the butter and sugar
- Mix in bananas, then flour, baking soda/powder, salt, and nuts
- Add to greased and floured pan
- Bake until brown/cracked, toothpick comes out clean
