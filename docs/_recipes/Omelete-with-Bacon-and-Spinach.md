---
layout: recipe
yield: 4
time: 1
tags:
- breakfast
ingredients:
- 8 large eggs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 8 slices bacon, chopped
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 cups baby spinach
directions:
- Cook bacon
- Beat eggs, salt, and pepper
- Leave 1 tbsp fat left, add onion and cook 5 min
- Stir in 1 cup spinach at a time and cook until wilted. (~ 1 min)
- Add egg mixture and bacon
---
