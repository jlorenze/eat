---

yield: 4
time: .25
tags:

ingredients:
- 2 zucchini
- 1 yellow summer squash
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- cherry tomatoes
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
directions:
- Toss veggies in olive oil and spice mix
- Spread evenly on baking sheet
- Broil until done. (~10 min)
