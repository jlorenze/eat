ingredients:
- 1 medium carrot
- 2 cups cauliflower
- 1 lb ground chicken
- 2 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 2/3 cup breadcrumbs
directions:
- Preheat oven to 375F, prep tray with parchment paper
- Finely shred or process cauliflower and carrot
- Add all ingredients together, minus breadcrumbs
- Form ball with 1 Tbsp mixture, coat in breadcrumbs, then flatten into nugget shape
- Optionally spray tops with a little bit of oil
- Bake on parchment paper for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway
