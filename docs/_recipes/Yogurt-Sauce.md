- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 Tbsp white wine vinegar
- 1 Tbsp chopped fresh dill

- black pepper

- Mix all ingredients, adding a little water to thin as needed
- Season with salt and pepper
