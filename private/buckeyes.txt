Buckeyes

Yield: 80-100

Time (hours):

Keywords: dessert

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
18 oz peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 lb powdered sugar
12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/4 cake paraffin wax

Make:
1. Cream together butter, peanut butter, and vanilla.
2. Gradually add powdered sugar.
3. Roll into balls, set on cookie sheets on wax paper, and chill.
4. In double boiler (or heavy bottom pan), melt chocolate and wax.
5. Dip balls in chocolate using a toothpick, set on wax paper and chill.

Notes:
