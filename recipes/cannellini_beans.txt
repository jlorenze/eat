Cannellini Beans

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 12

Keywords: italian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/3 cup EVOO
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed
1 lb dried cannellini beans, rinsed
1 cup chicken stock
herbs (bay leaf, thyme, parsley, rosemary)
salt

Make:
1. Soak beans for 8-24 hours with 2 inches water covering.
2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and cook onions and garlic for 5 minutes. 
3. Add beans, herbs, the water they soaked in, and chicken stock. Add more water to cover beans by 3 inches.
4. Bring to a boil (skim off foam), and simmer gently for about 2 hours.
5. When desired tenderness is reached, discard herb stems and season with salt to taste.
