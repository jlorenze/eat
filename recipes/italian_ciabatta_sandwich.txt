Italian Ciabatta Sandwich

Yield: 2-4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sandwich

Recommended Sides: white balsamic glaze, ciabatta

Ingredients:
ciabatta loaf
ham
salami
roasted red pepper
fresh mozzarella
fresh basil
arugula
white balasamic glaze
olive oil

Make:
1. Slice ciabatta loaf in half and toast in the oven for a few minutes.
2. Drizzle interior both sides of ciabatta with olive oil, and smear top half with balsamic glaze.
3. Stack basil, ham, salami, mozzarella, roasted red pepper, and arugula.
