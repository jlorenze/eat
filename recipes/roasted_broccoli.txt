Roasted Broccoli

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides: Roasted Broccoli with Feta

Ingredients:
1 large head broccoli
olive oil
salt
spices

Make:
1. Spread broccoli evenly on baking sheet and avoid overcrowding. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with desired spices.
2. Bake at 425F until roasted to desired level.

Notes:
1. See also Roasted Broccoli with Feta for a twist
1. Overcrowding the sheet pan will cause veggies to steam rather than roast.
