Garlic Bread

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: bread

Recommended Sides: ciabatta, simple baguette

Ingredients:
1 loaf ciabatta or french bread
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
3 Tbsp olive oil
6 cloves garlic, finely minced
1 Tbsp fresh parsley
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
sea salt

Make:
1. Whip garlic, butter, olive oil, and parsley. Spread bread with mixture, top with Parmesan, and sprinkle with salt.
2. Bake at 400F for 8-10 min, until edges of bread are just starting to brown.

Notes:
1. Could also make with garlic powder, just spread butter and olive oil mixture on bread and then sprinkle desired amount of garlic powder along with salt over top.
2. This quantity is sufficient for 2 loaves of the simple baguette recipe.
3. Try briefly sauteing the garlic (may need to increase amount of garlic).
