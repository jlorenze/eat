Pesto Grilled Cheese

Yield: 1

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sandwich

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
bread
basil pesto
provolone cheese
butter or margarine

Make:
1. Butter one side of each bread slice, spread basil pesto over the other sides.
2. Add provolone and cook in skillet until golden brown and cheese is melted.
