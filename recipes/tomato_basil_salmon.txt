Tomato Basil Salmon

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: seafood, meat

Recommended Sides: roasted asparagus, zuchini, Buttery Garlic Green Beans

Ingredients:
2 6oz salmon fillets
1 tbsp dried basil
1 tomato, thinly sliced
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 375F. Like baking sheet with foil, and spray with cooking spray.
2. Place salmon fillets onto foil, sprinkle with basil, top with tomato. Drizzle with oil, sprinkle with cheese.
3. Bake about 20 min, until salmon is done.
