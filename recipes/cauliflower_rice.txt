Cauliflower Rice

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides: Tex Mex Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1 tbsp olive oil
1 shallot, minced
1/2 cup chicken broth
salt and pepper
2 tbsp fresh parsely, minced

Make:
1. Pulse cauliflower until finely ground.
2. Heat oil in large pan over medium low heat, add shallot and cook until softened. (~ 3 min)
3. Stir in cauliflower, broth, and 1.5 tsp salt.
4. Cover and cook, until cauliflower is tender. (~12 - 15 min)
5. Uncover and cook until rice is almost dry. (~3 min)
6. Stir in parsely and season with salt and pepper.
