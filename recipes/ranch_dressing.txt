Ranch Dressing

Yield: 

Time (hours):

Keywords: sauces-toppings

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 Tbsp fresh dill
1 tsp lemon juice

Make:
1. Mix together and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving. Adjust seasonings as desired.

Notes:
1. Can replace spices with Hidden Valley ranch seasoning.
