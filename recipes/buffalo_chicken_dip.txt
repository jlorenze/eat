Buffalo Chicken Dip

Yield:

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: appetizer

Recommended Sides: saturday white bread, celery, crackers

Ingredients:
3-4 cups shredded cooked chicken
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/2-3/4 cup Frank's Red Hot
8 oz Colby-Monterey Jack cheese, shredded

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Mix all ingredients together, bake uncovered for 20-25 minutes or until cheese is melted.
