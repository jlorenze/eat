Dijon Turkey Burgers

Yield: 4

Time (hours): .75

Keywords: grill, meat

Recommended Sides: roasted red potatoes, Southern Green Beans, Seasoned Potato Wedges

Ingredients:
1/2 cup shredded carrot
1/4 cup scallions
2 tbsp milk
1/4 tsp dried Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1 lb ground turkey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp curry powder
lettuce
tomato
buns

Make:
1. Add all ingredients to bowl and mix well.
2. Form turkey into patties.
3. Place on greased pan and broil on high for 11-13 min, turning halfway.
