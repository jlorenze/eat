Lemon Chicken Orzo Soup

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.75

Keywords: soups-stews

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
4 tsp olive oil
1/2 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into small pieces
1 onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
2 carrots, diced
1/2 tsp dried thyme
6 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 cup orzo
2 large eggs
3 Tbsp lemon juice
salt
black pepper

Make:
1. Heat 2 tsp oil in a soup pot over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and add to pot until just cooked through (~5 min). Transfer chicken to dish and set aside.
2. Add remaining 2 tsp oil, onion, celery, carrot, and thyme and cook over med-high het until tender (~5 min).
3. Add 5 cups broth and bring to a boil.
4. Add orzo and simmer until tender (~8 min). Turn the heat down to low to maintain heat without boiling.
5. Warm 1 cup broth in small saucepan until hot but not boiling. In medium bowl, whisk eggs while gradually adding lemon juice.
6. Gradually whisk in the hot broth to the egg-lemon mixture.
7. Add mixture to soup, stirring well until soup thickens.
8. Add cooked chicken and season with salt and pepper.
