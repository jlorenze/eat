Hannah's Rolls

Yield: 12

Time (hours): overnight + 2

Keywords: bread

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 cups tepid water 
2 tbsp yeast
1/2 cup sugar 
2 tsp salt
1/4 cup oil (or melted butter) 
6 cups flour
1 egg

Make:
1. In bowl, proof yeast with 1 tbsp sugar.
2. Add salt and remaining sugar. Add oil, then mix in three cups of flour.
3. Mix in one egg, then add flour one cup at a time until dough pulls away from sides of bown. (Should be approx three more cups)
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Or on counter for 2 hours until doubled.
5. Two hours before baking, punch down dough and form into rolls.
6. Let dough rise for 2 hours.
7. Bake at 350F for 15 min until golden.
8. Lightly butter tops to prevent hardening.
