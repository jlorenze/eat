Chipotle Carnitas

Yield:

Time (hours): 10

Keywords: mexican, crockpot

Recommended Sides: chipotle cilantro lime rice, chipotle corn salsa, chipotle tomato salsa, black beans, guacamole

Ingredients:
whole pork shoulder
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cans Rotel tomatoes and chiles
2 Tbsp garlic, minced
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp ground cloves
2 tsp dried chipotle pepper
1 tsp cumin
1 cup beef broth
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 bay leaves

Make:
1. Heat oil in large pan and brown pork on all sides.
2. Add pork and all ingredients in slow cooker.
3. Cook on low 8-10 hours.
4. Remove pork and shred.
5. Remove bay leaves, drain half liquid.
6. Add shredded pork back to crock pot.
