Braised Green Beans with Potatoes and Basil

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 1.5

Keywords: soups-stews

Recommended Sides: Overnight Whole Wheat Bread

Ingredients:
5 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped fine
2 tsp dried oregano
4 garlic cloves, minced
1.5 cups water
1.5 lbs green beans, trimmed
1.5 lbs Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 tsp baking soda
15 oz can diced tomatoes
2 Tbsp tomato paste
salt
pepper
3 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
lemon juice

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 275F.
2. In dutch oven heat 3 Tbsp oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened (~5 min). Stir in oregano and garlic and cook until fragrant.
3. Stir in water, green beans, potatoes, and baking soda. Simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Stir in tomatoes and their juices, tomato paste, 2.5 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper.
5. Cover dutch oven with lid and bake in oven until green beans are softened (~40-50 min).
6. Stir in basil, add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
7. Serve with remaining 2 Tbsp oil drizzled over top.

Notes:
1. Without dutch oven, cook on stove-top in large pot and simmer at low temp instead of baking.
