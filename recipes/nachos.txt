Nachos

Yield: 2-6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mexican, appetizer

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
tortilla chips
Cheddar cheese
Monterey Jack cheese
Oaxaca cheese
cooked seasoned ground beef
pinto beans
refried beans
tomato
red onion
jalapenos
cilantro
sour cream
guacamole

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
3. Stack desired ingredients in layers in cast iron skillet.
4. Bake for 15 min, top with cilantro and serve with sour cream and guacamole.

Notes:
1. Put cheese on first to protect chips from moisture.
