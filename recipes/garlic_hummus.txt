Garlic Hummus

Yield: 6 to 8

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mediterranean

Recommended Sides: bread, pita, carrots, eggplant roasted tomato hummus, roasted red bell hummus

Ingredients:
2 15 oz cans chickpeas, drained, liquid reserved
2 cloves garlic, minced (not 4)
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cumin
1/3 cup tahini, stirred well
5 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
paprika to taste

Make:
1. Place chickpeas in food processor with garlic, salt, cumin. Process 30 seconds. Add tahini, process more.
2. Add lemon juice and 1/4 cup reserved bean liquid, process.
3. Add olive oil, process.
