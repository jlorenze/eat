Garlic Hummus

Yield: 6 to 8

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mediterranean

Recommended Sides: bread, pita, carrots, eggplant roasted tomato hummus, roasted red bell hummus

Ingredients:
1/2 lb dried chickpeas 
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cumin
1/3 cup tahini, stirred well
5 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
paprika to taste

Make:
1. Cook chickpeas. <chickpeas>
2. Place chickpeas in food processor with garlic, salt, cumin. Process 30 seconds. Add tahini and lemon juice, process more.
3. Add olive oil, process.

Notes:
1. Can replace dried chickpeas with 30 oz canned chickpeas, drained.
