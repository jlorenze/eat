Roasted Cauliflower

Yield:

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
cauliflower
olive oil
salt
pepper
spices (crushed red pepper flakes, smoked paprika, turmeric, etc.)

Make:
1. Toss cauliflower with olive oil and desired spices. Spread evenly on baking sheet and avoid overcrowding.
2. Bake at 425 F for 30 min or until done.

Notes:
1. Overcrowding the sheet pan will cause veggies to steam rather than roast.
