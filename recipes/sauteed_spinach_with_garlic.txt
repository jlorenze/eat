Sauteed Spinach with Garlic

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
12 oz baby spinach leaves, rinsed and dried
1-2 Tbsp olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 Tbsp unsalted butter

Make:
1. Heat oil in large pot or pan over medium low heat. Saute garlic, but do not brown.
2. Add spinach, salt, and pepper. Toss and cook until spinach is wilted.
3. Top with butter before serving.
