Vegetable Chowder

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: vegetables, soups-stews

Recommended Sides: sandwiches

Ingredients:
2 lb potatoes, chunked
1 onion, chopped
1 small bag frozen corn
2 carrots, diced
2 celery stalks, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 crown broccoli
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tbsp thyme, chopped
1/8 tsp pepper
2 tsp ground cumin
3 tbsp dill, chopped
salt

Make:
1. Combine all veggies, garlic, thyme, pepper, cumin, and 6 cups of water to large pot.
2. Bring to boil, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 30 min or until veggies are tender.
3. Remove 1-2 cups of soup and puree in blender.
4. Add pureed back to pot along with dill. Season with salt.
