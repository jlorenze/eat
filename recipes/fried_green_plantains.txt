Fried Green Plantain Chips

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetarian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 large green plantains
vegetable oil
salt

Make:
1. Peel plantains and slice into thin rounds.
2. Fill large skillet with enough vegetable oil to cover the chips, ~1/2 inch.
3. Fry plantains in hot oil (over med to med-high heat), turning occasionally.
4. Drain on rack or paper towel and sprinkle with salt.

Notes:
1. Sprinkling salt immediately after removing from the oil will help it stick better.
