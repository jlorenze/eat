Baked Salmon

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: seafood, meat

Recommended Sides: roasted asparagus, zucchini

Ingredients:
2 (6 oz) salmon fillets
1 tbsp olive oil
salt and pepper
lemon slices

Make:
1. Coat salmon with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Bake 450 F for 12-15 min.
3. Serve with lemon.
