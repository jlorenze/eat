Baked Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Yield: 6

Time (hours): .75

Keywords: italian, meat

Recommended Sides: pasta, salad, asparagus, roasted tomato, broccoli

Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup dry bread crumbs
2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp pepper
6 skinless boneless chicken breast halves

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease 9x13 baking dish.
2. Dip chicken breasts into oil mixture, then crumb mixture.
3. Bake 30 minutes or until chicken is done.
