Thai Pork with Peanut Sauce

Yield:

Time (hours):

Keywords: crockpot, asian, meat

Recommended Sides: rice, egg noodles

Ingredients:
2 lb boneless pork loin
1 large red bell pepper, strips
1/3 cup teriyaki sauce
2 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tsp red pepper flakes
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup green onion
1/4 cup dry roasted peanuts

Make:
1. Everything besides peanut butter, green onion, peanuts in slow cooker.
2. Cook on low for 8-9 hours.
3. Remove pork and chop.
4. Add peanut butter to juices in slow cooker, stir till smooth.
5. Return meat to sauce and toss to coat.
6. Serve and sprinkle with onions and peanuts.
