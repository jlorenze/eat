Buffalo Chicken Fajitas

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: american

Recommended Sides: corn, roasted cauliflower

Ingredients:
2 Tbsp butter
1/3 cup Frank's RedHot sauce
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 lb cooked chicken breast
1 large yellow onion, sliced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
flour tortillas
monterey jack cheese, shredded
sour cream
fresh cilantro

Make:
1. Melt butter, stir in hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
2. Heat a large cast iron skillet over med-high heat. Add oil and saute onions and bell peppers for a couple minutes.
3. Add in mushrooms and continue to saute until tender
4. Add chicken and stir in buffalo sauce until heated through.
5. Serve with cheese, sour cream, and cilantro.
