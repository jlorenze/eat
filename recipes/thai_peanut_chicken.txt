Thai Peanut Chicken

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 4

Keywords: crockpot, asian, meat

Recommended Sides: cauliflower rice, rice, egg noodles

Ingredients:
1.5 lb chicken breast tenderloins
1 red pepper, diced
1 onion, chopped
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 lime
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 tbsp cumin
1/2 tbsp corriander
scallions, chopped
cilantro, chopped
bean sprouts
peanuts

Make:
1. Add chicken, red pepper, and onion to slow cooker.
2. Whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, chicken broth, cumin, and coriander. Pour over chicken.
3. Cook on high for 4 hours or until chicken is done.
4. Serve with scallions, cilantro, bean sprouts, and crushed peanuts.
