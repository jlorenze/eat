Roasted Broccoli with Feta

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 large head broccoli, cut into large florets
1/3 cup olive oil
salt
pepper
crushed red pepper flakes
1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1/2 cup crumbled feta

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 425F with rack in middle.
2. Toss broccoli with oil and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes.
3. Heat cast-iron skillet over med-high heat, add broccoli, and cook without stirring until bottom begins to brown. Flip and repeat.
4. Transfer to oven and roast for 5 minutes or until desired level of browning.
5. Remove from oven and drizze with red wine vinegar and feta.
