Roasted Vegetables and Sausage

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: meat, american, vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 lb red potatoes
8 oz baby carrots
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
8 oz asparagus
1/2 large yellow onion
1 red bell pepper
1 package skinless smoked sausage
1 Tbsp minced garlic
fresh parsley

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 400F.
2. Cube potatoes into 1 inch chunks. Toss potatoes and carrots with half of spices and olive oil.
3. Spread onto sheet pans and bake for 20 min.
4. Cut asparagus into 2 inch pieces, and slice onion, bell pepper, and sausage. Toss in remaining oil, spices, and minced garlic.
5. Add to sheet pans and bake for another 10-15 minutes.
6. Toss with fresh parsley.

Notes:
1. Be sure to spread out veggies as much as possible to ensure they roast instead of steaming.
