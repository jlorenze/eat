Overnight Rolled Oats

Yield: 1

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: breakfast

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled (old-fashioned) oats
1/2 milk of choice
1/4 cup greek yogurt
1 Tbsp chia seeds
1 Tbsp sweetener
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
salt
pb + jelly + fruit
apples + pecans + cinnamon
banana + nutella
coconut + almonds + chocolate
blueberries + lemon zest + honey
brown sugar + maple syrup + cinnamon
protein powder

Make:
1. Place oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, sweetener, extact, and some salt in a jar and mix until combined.
2. Refrigerator overnight.
3. Top with desired extras, can thin with a little more milk or water as desired.

Notes:
1. Store in fridge for up to 5 days.
