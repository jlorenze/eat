Reuben Sandwich

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sandwich

Recommended Sides: raw vegetables, soups, salads

Ingredients:
8 slices rye bread
butter
1 lb corned beef
swiss cheese
sauerkraut, pressed to remove juices
tomato, sliced (optional)
Thousand Island dressing

Make:
1. Top buttered bread with corned beef, cheese, sauerkraut, and tomatoes.
2. Grill over medium heat on each side until bread is toasted and cheese is melted.
3. Add desired amount of dressing onto the sandwich, and serve with extra dressing on the side for dipping.

Notes:
1. For a quick homemade Thousand Island, mix 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 2 Tbsp ketchup, 2 Tbsp sweet relish, 1/4 tsp onion powder, and a dash of Worcestershire sauce.
2. Cut it diagonally or don't cut it at all.
