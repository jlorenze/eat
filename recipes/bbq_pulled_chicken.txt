BBQ Pulled Chicken

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 2.5

Keywords: crockpot, meat, american

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1.75 cups barbecue sauce
1/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
salt
pepper
2.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast

Make:
1. In slow cooker combine all ingredients except chicken and mix. Add chicken and coat well.
2. Cover and cook on high for 2 hours or low for 4 hours.
3. Remove chicken and transfer BBQ sauce to a sauce pan. Bring sauce to a boil over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes to reduce to desired consistency.
4. Shred the chicken, pour sauce over top and toss to coat.

Notes:
1. Kinder's mild BBQ sauce is good.
