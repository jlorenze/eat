Greek Pasta Salad

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: mediterranean, pasta, salad

Recommended Sides: greek salad dressing

Ingredients:
16 oz box vegetable rotini noodles, cooked
1/4 cup mild banana peppers, chopped
1/2 cup kalamata olives, chopped
2-4 oz crumbled feta cheese
grape tomatoes, halved
cucumber, chopped
greek salad dressing

Make:
1. Mix all ingredients. Add 3/4 to 1 cup greek salad dressing.
2. Refrigerate, may need to add more dressing before serving.
