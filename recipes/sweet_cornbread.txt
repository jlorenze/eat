Sweet Cornbread

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: bread, american

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
1.5 cups milk
2.5 cups flour
1 cup corn meal
4.5 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

Make:
1. Combine dry and wet.
2. Bake at 400F for 20-27 minutes in a greased 13x9 baking dish.

Notes:
1. For 8x8 baking dish just halve the ingredients.
