Pumpkin Cream Cheese Muffins

Yield: 12

Time (hours): 0.75

Keywords: bread, breakfast, american

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup white whole wheat flour
3/4 cups unbleached AP flour
3/4 cup raw sugar
1 tsp baking soda
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1.5 cups canned pumpkin (not pie filling)
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 large egg
1.5 tsp vanilla extract
5 oz cream cheese
2 Tbsp raw sugar
1 large egg yolk
1 tsp vanilla extract

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F, lightly spray muffin tin with oil.
2. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking soda, and spices in a medium bowl.
3. In a large bowl mix pumpkin puree, oil, whole egg, and 1.5 tsp vanilla extract. Beat at medium speed until thick.
4. Add flour mixture to wet mixture, mix until combined and do not over mix.
5. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese, sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla extract, then beat until smooth.
6. Pour batter into muffin tins, drop 1 Tbsp cream cheese mixture onto the tops and swirl into the batter.
7. Bake on center rack until toothpick test passes (~24-26 min).
8. Let cool before serving.

Notes:
1. Refrigerate muffins for a couple days.
