Bagel Egg Cheese Breakfast Sandwich

Yield: 1

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: breakfast, sandwich

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 bagel, sliced and toasted
2 eggs
cream cheese
Sriracha
salt
pepper
bacon or ham (optional)
arugula (optional)

Make:
1. Cook eggs as preferred, season to taste with salt and pepper.
2. Spread cream cheese on bagel and add Sriracha to taste.
3. Assemble sandwich with other desired ingredients.

Notes:
1. Can replace cream cheese with sliced cheese.
