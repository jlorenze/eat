Snickerdoodle Cookies

Yield: 12

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: dessert

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
3/4 cup sugar
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 3/8 cup flour
3/4 tsp cream of tartar
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 cup sugar
3/4 Tbsp cinnamon

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Cream butter and sugar for 4-5 min until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla, then cream for 1-2 minutes longer.
3. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt, just until combined.
4. In small bowl, mix remaining 1/8 cup sugar and cinnamon.
5. Wrap dough and refrigerate for 20-30 minutes (optional).
6. Roll dough into smooth balls and coat well with cinnamon mixture.
7. Place balls on parchment paper-lined baking sheet and press down in the center of the ball with your thumb.
8. Bake for 9-11 minutes, until the edges just begin to turn a light golden color. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes and then transfer to wire rack.
