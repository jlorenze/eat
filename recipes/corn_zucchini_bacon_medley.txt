Corn, Zucchini, and Bacon Medley

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords:

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
2 zucchini
1 yellow summer squash
1 package frozen corn
1/2 onion, chopped
pepper

Make:
1. Cook bacon in large skillet. Reserve 1 tbsp bacon grease in pan.
2. Add vegetables, cook until zucchini is crisp and tender. (~10 - 13 min)
3. Season with pepper, sprinkle with crumbled bacon.
