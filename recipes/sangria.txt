Sangria

Yield:

Time (hours):

Keywords: drinks

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 bottle Pinot Noir or Garnacha
1 orange, sliced, halved
1 lemon, sliced, halved
1 lime, sliced, halved
1-2 Tbsp maple syrup
1/2 cup brandy
orange juice (optional)

Make:
1. Muddle fruit and maple syrup.
2. Add orange juice and brandy, muddle.
3. Add red wine and ice for serving.
