Sandwich Buns

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 3

Keywords: bread, sandwich

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
420g Unbleached AP flour
220g lukewarm water
4 Tbsp butter
1 large egg
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1.25 tsp salt
1 Tbsp instant yeast

Make:
1. Mix and knead all ingredients, except 2 Tbsp butter, by hand or in a mixer.
2. Cover and let rise until doubled (1-2 hours).
3. Divide and shape into 8 balls, then flatten each do make 3 inch diameter disks.
4. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet, cover, and let rise until puffy (~1 hour).
5. Brush buns with 1 Tbsp melted butter and bake for 15-18 minutes at 375F.
6. Brush with remaining 1 Tbsp melted butter, then cool on a wire rack.

Notes:
1. Store, well-wrapped, at room temperature for several days. Can also freeze for longer storage.
2. Maybe try without the sugar next time
