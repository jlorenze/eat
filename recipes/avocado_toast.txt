Avocado Toast

Category: breakfast

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: american

Recommended Sides: broccoli, veggies

Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 avocado
1/4 cup salsa
1 tsp garlic salt
2 slices heavy whole wheat bread

Prep:

Make:
1. Heat pan to med, cook eggs over-easy to over-med.
2. Make guac with avocado, salsa, garlic salt.
3. Lightly toast bread.
