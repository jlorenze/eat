Pickled Red Onions

Yield: 1 cup

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: sauces-toppings

Recommended Sides: black beans on toast

Ingredients:
1 red onion, halved and sliced thin
1 cup red wine vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
2 jalapeno chiles, stemmed, seeded, and cut into thin rings
1/4 tsp salt

Make:
1. Bring vinegar, sugar, jalapenos, and salt to simmer, cook until sugar dissolves.
2. Pour over onion, cover and let cool for 1 hour.
3. Drain in colander and serve or refrigerate for up to 1 week.
