Buffalo Chicken Nachos

Yield: 2-6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: american, appetizer

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/2 lb cooked chicken, shredded
2 Tbsp butter, melted
1/4 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
tortilla chips
1 large tomato, diced
1/2 red onion, finely diced
blue cheese, crumbled
Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
scallions
sour cream

Make:
1. Toss chicken in mixture of melted butter and Frank's.
2. Stack ingredients in two layers in cast iron skillet.
3. Bake for 15 min, top with scallions and serve with sour cream.

Notes:
1. Makes 2 full sized dinner portions, or 6 for an appetizer size portion.
2. Using store roasted, shredded chicken saves a lot of time.
