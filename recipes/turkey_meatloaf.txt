Turkey Meatloaf

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 1.5

Keywords: american, meat

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 large shallot, minced
2 lbs ground turkey
2/3 cup chopped cremini mushrooms
3/4 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1/3 cup ketchup
2 large eggs
1.25 tsp salt
1 tsp dried marjoram or oregano
1 oz chopped fresh baby spinach
2 Tbsp ketchup
2 tsp brown sugar
1.5 tsp dijon mustard
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F, coat 9x13 inch baking dish with oil spray.
2. Saute shallot in a bit of olive oil until golden.
3. Combine shallot, turkey, mushrooms, bread crumbs, 1/3 cup ketchup, eggs, salt, marjoram, and spinach until well blended.
4. Pat mixture firmly into a 10x12 inch rectange in baking dish.
5. Bake 45 min.
6. Combine 2 Tbsp ketchup, brown sugar, dijon mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
7. Remove loaf from oven and brush sauce mixture over top, return to oven for 10-15 minutes.
8. Remove from oven and allow to rest for 10 min.
