Roasted Zucchini, Squash, and Cherry Tomatoes

Yield: 4

Time (hours): .25

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 zucchini
1 yellow summer squash
1/2 onion, chopped
cherry tomatoes
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Make:
1. Toss veggies in olive oil and spice mix.
2. Spread evenly on baking sheet.
3. Broil until done. (~10 min)
