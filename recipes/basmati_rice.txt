Basmati Rice

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: indian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 cup basmati rice
1.75 cups water
1.5 Tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 tsp salt

Make:
1. Soak rice in cold water for 15 min, gently stirring to release excess starch. Drain.
2. Bring 1.75 cups water, butter, salt, and rice to a boil. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes until rice is tender.
3. Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
