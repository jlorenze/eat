Pumpkin Pancakes

Yield: 12

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: breakfast

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
3 Tbsp brown sugar
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
2 Tbsp vinegar
1 cup pumpkin
1.5 cups milk

Make:
1. Combine dry and wet ingredients in separate bowls.
2. Mix wet into dry.
3. Griddle in desired sizes.
