Classic Chicken Salad

Yield: 6-8

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: american, salad

Recommended Sides: crackers, fresh bread

Ingredients:
4 cups cooked chicken
1 cup mayonnaise
2 stalks celery, chopped fine
3 green onions, chopped fine
2 tsp dijon mustard
1 tsp salt
black pepper
2 tsp fresh dill

Make:
1. If needed, bake chicken and let cool. <baked_chicken>
2. Combine all ingredients and mix well.
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
