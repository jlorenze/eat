Corn Salsa

Yield: 

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mexican, appetizer

Recommended Sides: chipotle carnitas, cilantro lime rice, chipotle grilled chicken, chipotle sofritas, pico de gallo, black beans, guacamole

Ingredients:
2 cans white or yellow sweet corn (low sodium)
1/2 cup red onion, chopped (a little less)
2 jalapeno peppers, stemmed, seeded, chopped
1/4 cup roasted Poblano peppers, chopped (2 poblanos)
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp fresh lime juice

Make:
1. Set broiler high, line baking sheet with foil, place peppers, spray with cooking spray.
2. Broil 6 min, flip, and broil 6 min.
3. Remove and place in covered bowl for 10 min to steam.
4. Spray fingers with cooking spray to protect, remove skin, stem, and seeds.
5. Combine ingredients, add salt to taste (maybe 1/2 to 1 tsp).
