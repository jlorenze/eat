Bagel Breakfast Sandwich

Yield: 1

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: breakfast, sandwich

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 bagel, sliced
2 eggs
bacon, sausage, ham, or other meat
american, jack, cream, or other cheese
mayo
Sriracha
arugula or other greens

Make:
1. Cook meat and scramble eggs. Season eggs with salt and pepper.
2. Toast bagel.
3. Assemble with desired toppings.

Notes:
