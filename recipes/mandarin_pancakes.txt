Mandarin Pancakes

Yield: 12

Time (hours): 1.5

Keywords: asian, bread

Recommended Sides: mu shoo chicken

Ingredients:
1.5 cups all-purpose flour
1/8 tsp salt
2/3 cup boiling water
1 tsp oil

Make:
1. Mix flour in salt in heatproof bowl. Pour boiling water and mix until ball forms.
2. Knead dough for 8 minutes until smooth, adding flour if too sticky. Cover and rest for 1 hour.
3. Roll into a cylinder and cut into 12 equal pieces. Roll into a ball then flatten into a disk of ~2-inch diameter.
4. Brush tops and sides of 6 with oil, place remaining 6 on top to make 6 double-stacked disks with oil in between.
5. Roll into 7-inch circles, using small amount of flour to keep from sticking. Flip frequently for even rolling.
6. Heat skillet over medium heat, cook pancakes one at at time, about 30-45 seconds per side. Let cool 30 seconds and then pull the two pancakes apart.
7. Cover cooked pancakes with a warm kitchen towel.

Notes:
1. A couple of faint brown patches are fine, but too much brown is overcooked.
