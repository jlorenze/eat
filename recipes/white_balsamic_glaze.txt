White Balsamic Glaze

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: Sauces-Toppings

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup white balsamic vinegar
1 tsp honey

Make:
1. Whisk vinegar and honey in small sauce pot and bring to simmer over medium-high heat.
2. Turn heat down to medium and slow simmer until mixture coats a spoon (~7-10 min). Remove from heat.

Notes:
1. Once the glaze begins to thicken it will happen quite fast.
