Seasoned Potato Wedges

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides: Black Bean Quinoa Burger

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp salt
2 russet potatoes

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 425F.
2. Place seasoning, olive oil, and potatoes into plastic bag and shake.
3. Bake in oven until potatoes are tender. (~ 25 min)
