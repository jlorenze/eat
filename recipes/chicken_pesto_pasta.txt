Chicken Pesto Pasta

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: pasta, italian

Recommended Sides: salad, roasted vegetables

Ingredients:
1 lb cooked chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
8 oz penne pasta
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes, drained
1/2 cup basil pesto
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Cook pasta in salted water, cook until al dente. Reserve 1 cup cooking water.
2. Combine cooked pasta with other ingredients and salt and pepper to taste. Add enough reserved pasta cooking water to make the sauce have the right consistency.
