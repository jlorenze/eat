Indian Basmati Rice

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: indian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1.5 cups basmati rice
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/8 tsp ground cardamom
1.5 tsp salt
2.5 cups water

Make:
1. Soak rice in cold water for 20 minutes, then drain thoroughly.
2. Heat vegetable oil over med-high heat. Add cinnamon, cumin, cloves, and cardamom and stirl until combined.
3. Add rice and stir to coat, saute for 2 min.
4. Add salt and water and bring to boil. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Remove from heat and fluff.

Notes:
1. A fast/simple variation on basmatic rice.
