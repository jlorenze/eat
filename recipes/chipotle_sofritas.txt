Chipotle Sofritas

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mexican

Recommended Sides: cilantro lime rice, corn salsa, pico de gallo, black beans, guacamole

Ingredients:
1 poblano pepper, roasted
3 chipotle peppers, canned in adobo sauce
2 tbsp adobo sauce
2 garlic cloves
3/4 cup salsa
1 tbsp agave
salt and pepper
2 tbsp avocado or canola oil
1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed, crumbled
1 cup corn

Make:
1. Add all ingredients except oil, tofu, corn to food processor and blend till smooth.
2. Heat large, nonstick pan over med eat with 1 tbsp oil.
3. Add tofu, corn, cook 10 min until tofu browns.
4. Add sauce, cover, cook 5 min.
