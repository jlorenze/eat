Southern Green Beans

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, finely diced
1 tsp salt
2 cloves garlic, sliced
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
1 cup unsalted chicken broth
1 lb green beans, trimmed

Make:
1. Heat oil of med-high heat, add onion and 1/2 tsp salt.
2. Cook 2 min and add garlic and red pepper for another min.
3. Add broth and remaining salt, bring to simmer and add green beans.
4. Cover and simmer 20 min or until beans are tender, remove from pot.
5. Boil remaining liquid until reduced to 1/4 cup, drizzle over green beans.
