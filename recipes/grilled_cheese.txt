Classic Grilled Cheese

Yield: 1

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sandwich

Recommended Sides: soups

Ingredients:
sliced bread (potato, whole grain, white)
butter or margarine
sliced cheese (American, cheddar, Colby, provolone, or Monterey Jack)

Make:
1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
2. Spread butter or margarine on one side of each bread slice. Place cheese on bread and grill until toasty brown. Flip and repeat.
