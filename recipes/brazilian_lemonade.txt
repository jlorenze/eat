Brazilian Lemonade

Yield: 4-6

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: drinks

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
4 juicy limes
4 cups cold water
14 oz can sweet condensed milk
ice

Make:
1. Wash limes and peel two of them, cut into halves.
2. Combine lime wedges and water in blender and pulse for no more than 10 seconds.
3. Strain with fine-mesh strainer, pressing pulp to extract all juice.
4. Return to blender with condensed milk, blend until creamy and frothy.
5. Add desired amount of ice and blend again.

Notes:
1. Can garnish with lime wedges and mint.
