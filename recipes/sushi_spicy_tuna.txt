Sushi ( Spicy Tuna Roll )

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: asian, seafood

Recommended Sides: sushi rice, Sushi ( California Roll ), Sushi ( Philly Roll ), Sushi ( Vegetarian Roll )

Ingredients:
sushi rice
5 oz can tuna in water
1 tbsp sriracha
1/2 tsp chili oil
1/2 tsp rice vinegar
2 tbsp scallions, finely chopped
1.5 tbsp mayonnaise
cucumber
shredded carrot
sesame seeds

Make:
1. Combine tuna, sriracha, chili oil, rice vinegar, scallions, and mayonnaise.
2. Roll it up!
