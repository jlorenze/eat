Buffalo Chicken Salad

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: american, salad

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 hearts romaine lettuce
spinach
3 shredded carrots
5 celery, chopped
1 red bell, chopped
1 green bell, chopped
1 cucumber
tomato
blue cheese crumbles
1 lb chicken (thighs or breast)
1 tbsp oil
2 tbsp butter
1/4 cup buffalo sauce
ranch dressing

Make:
1. Preheat cast iron to med-high.
2. Add oil and butter to pan with chicken, sear 2-3 min then add hot sauce. Cook until done.
3. Roughly chop and combine veggies.
4. Roughly chop lettuce and combine with spinach.
5. Top with desired amount of ranch dressing, extra buffalo sauce, and blue cheese.
