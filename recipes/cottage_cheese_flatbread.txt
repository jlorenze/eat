Cottage Cheese Flatbread

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: bread

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 cup AP flour
2 tsp salt
2 Tbsp baking powder
3/4 cup whipped cottage cheese

Make:
1. Mix flour, salt, and baking powder until well combined.
2. Mix in cottage cheese until a thick dough is formed. Can add extra flour or a little water if needed.
3. Knead by hand until smooth.
4. Divide into four balls, flatten to 1/4 inch thickness.
5. Cook in skillet over medium heat, 2-3 minutes per side.

Notes:
1. Its fine with regular and not whipped cottage cheese.
2. Store in fridge, or freeze for up to two months.
