Mushroom Kale Lasagna

Yield: 2-3

Time (hours): 1.25

Keywords: italian, pasta, vegetarian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
10 lasagna noodles, cooked
2.5 cups marinara sauce
1 tsp olive oil
2 coves garlic, minced
5 cups kale, chopped fine
8 oz mushrooms, chopped fine
15 oz ricotta cheese
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
1 egg, whisked
salt
pepper
3 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Ladle 1 cup sauce onto bottom of 9x13 baking dish.
3. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until golden (~1 min). Add kale, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp pepper and saute 5 min.
4. Add mushrooms, cook until soft (~5 min).
5. Combine kale, mushrooms, ricotta, Parmesan, egg, and some more salt and pepper in a medium bowl.
6. Spread out the cooked lasagna noodles on a piece of wax paper, add 1/3 cup of mushroom-kale mixture to each noodle. Then, roll up and place seam side down into baking dish.
7. Ladle remaining sauce over the noodles and top with mozzarella cheese.
8. Cover dish in foil, bake for 40 minutes.

Notes:
1. Try making this as a normal lasagna rather than rolls.
