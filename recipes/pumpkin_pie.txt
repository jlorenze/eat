Pumpkin Pie

Yield: 1

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: dessert

Recommended Sides: whipped cream, vanilla ice cream

Ingredients:
pie crust
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp cloves
2 eggs
15 oz can pumpkin
12 oz can evaporated milk

Make:
1. Combine all filling ingredients.
2. Bake at 425F for 15-20 minutes, then at 375F for 45-55 minutes.
3. Chill in refrigerator.
