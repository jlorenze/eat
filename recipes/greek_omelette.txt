Greek Omelette

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: breakfast, vegetarian

Recommended Sides: hearty toast, balsamic salad

Ingredients:
5 large eggs
feta cheese, chopped
kalamata olives, chopped
spinach, chopped
cherry tomatoes, halved
salt
pepper
olive oil

Make:
1. Cook cherry tomatoes cut side down over medium heat in a drizzle of oil until softened and starting to brown, set aside.
2. Scramble eggs and add to pan until just beginning to set, season with salt and pepper.
3. Add cheese, olives, spinach, and tomatoes and fold.
