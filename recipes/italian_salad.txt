Italian Salad

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: salad

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
iceberg lettuce
spinach
cherry tomatoes, halved
red onion, thinly sliced
seasoned croutons
black olives
pepperoncini peppers
parmesan cheese, shredded
3/4 cup vegetable oil
4 Tbsp red wine vinegar
2 tsp dijon mustard
3/4 tsp Italian seasoning
1.5 tsp sugar
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Wash and dry vegetables.
2. Combine vegetable oil, red wine vinegar, mustard, Italian seasonings, sugar, and some salt and pepper in a jar with lid. Shake well to combine. Refrigerate for an hour before serving.
3. Mix salad ingredients in desired quantities and serve with dressing.

Notes:
1. Add some fresh parsley or basil as well!
