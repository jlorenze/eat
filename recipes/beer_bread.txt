Beer Bread

Yield: 1 loaf

Time (hours):1.25

Keywords: bread

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
3 cups AP flour
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup sugar
12 oz beer
1/4 cup butter, melted

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 375F.
2. Mix dry ingredients until well combined.
3. Add beer and mix just enough to get dry ingredients moistened.
4. Pour into greased loaf pan and pour melted butter over top.
5. Bake 50-60 minutes.

Notes:
1. Can add shredded cheese, herbs, etc. as well.
