Overnight Steel Cut Oats

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: breakfast

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups steel cut oats
3 cups water
3 cups milk
1/2 tsp salt
peanut butter
bananas
cinnamon
strawberries
nutella
maple syrup
apples
pecans

Make:
1. Bring water, milk, and salt to boil.
2. Stir in oats and mix 2-3 minutes.
3. Remove from heat, cover, and leave overnight at room temperature.
4. Top with favorite toppings.

Notes:
1. Can use other types of milk, such as almond or soy.
