Coconut Rice

Yield: 5

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords:

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 cup long-grain white rice
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 cup water
1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
salt and pepper

Make:
1. Rinse rice under cold water in strainer until water runs clear.
2. Heat oil in heavy bottomed medium pot over med heat, add rice and sautee to coat with oil.
3. Add water and coconut milk, bring to simmer, reduce heat and cover.
4. Cook for 12 to 15 minutes.
5. Allow rice to rest for 5 minutes, fluff.
