Carrot Ginger Soup

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: soups-stews

Recommended Sides: bread, cheese

Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced
2 in. piece fresh ginger, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, smashed
2 lbs carrots, chopped
5 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
1/2 tsp baking soda
salt
pepper
lemon

Make:
1. Heat oil over medium-low heat. Add onions, ginger, and garlic and cook until onions are softened (~8 min).
2. Add carrots, broth, and baking soda and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil over high heat.
3. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until carrots are tender (~20 min).
4. Process soup until smooth. Add a splash of lemon juice to brighten flavor, and adjust other seasonings as desired.

Notes:
1. Can be served hot or cold.
2. Ginger can be omitted, if desired.
