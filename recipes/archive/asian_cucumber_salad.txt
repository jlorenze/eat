Asian Cucumber Salad

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: asian, salad

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
3 cups cucumber peeled, seeded, and sliced
1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp rice vinegar
1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
2 tsp honey
2 Tbsp chopped cilantro or sliced green onion
1 Tbsp sesame seeds
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Place cucumbers, red onion, and red bell pepper in a large bowl.
2. In small bowl, whisk together remaining ingredients.
3. Pour dressing over vegetables and toss to coat. Serve immediately or store in refrigerator for up to 8 hours.
