Chicken Salad with Bacon and Tomatoes

Yield: 4 to 6

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: meat, salad

Recommended Sides: paleo wraps,

Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
8 slices bacon
1 shallot (red onion?)
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp cider vinegar
2 tsp Dijon mustard
Pinch cayenne pepper
12 oz cherry tomatoes, quartered
2 celery ribs, minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Cook bacon in skillet.
2. Cook chicken in a bit of the bacon grease.
3. Add shallot and garlic to leftover grease, cook until softened. (~ 2 min)
4. Transfer to large bowl and let cool slightly, add oil, vinegar, mustard, cayenne, and 1/4 tsp salt.
5. Pat chicken dry and cube. Add chicken, tomatoes, celery, basil, and crisp bacon to dressing and toss. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
