Shrimp Fajitas

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: seafood, mexican

Recommended Sides: roasted cauliflower, mexican rice, beans, corn, green beans

Ingredients:
1 lb medium shrimp
1 tbsp fresh cilantro
olive oil
3 tsp Caribbean jerk seasoning
1/8 tsp chili powder
1/8 tsp ground cumin
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
2 zucchini
tortillas
salsa
sour cream
avocado

Make:
1. Toss shrimp with cilantro, 1 tbsp oil, and spices. Let stand 10 min.
2. In cast iron skillet heat a bit of oil over med-hig heat, add onion, peppers, zucchini.
3. Cook until crisp-tender, remove from pan.
4. Add bit of oil, add shrimp. Cook until shrimp are pink and done.
5. Return veggies to pan to heat through.
