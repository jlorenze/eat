Zucchini Spaghetti and Meatballs

Category: Main

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 1.5

Keywords: italian

Recommended Sides: salad, bread

Ingredients:
1/4 cup raw cashews
3 tbsp olive oil
2 onions, chopped
6 garlic cloves, minced
1 tbsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1/4 cup + 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil (1 Tbsp dried)
1 large egg
salt and pepper
1 lb 85% lean ground beef
2 lbs tomatoes, cored and quartered
1/4 cup tomato paste
3 lbs zucchini or yellow squash, trimmed

Make:
1. Heat oven to 375 deg, middle rack.
2. Put zucchini noodles on baking sheet and roast until tender. (~ 20-25 min)
3. Bring 4 cups water to boil and add cashews. Cook 15 minutes, drain and rinse.
4. Heat 1 tbsp oil, add onions and cook until lightly brown. (~8 minutes)
5. Stir in garlic, oregano, and pepper flakes. Transfer half of onion mixture to bowl and set aside.
6. Process remaining onion mixture (like 1 cup max), boiled cashews, 1/4 cup basil, egg, and 1.5 tsp salt to a paste.
7. In large bowl add paste and ground beef until combined. Roll into 1.5 in. meatballs.
8. Process tomatoes until smooth. 
9. Heat 1 tbsp oil in now empty skillet, brown meatballs on all sides. (~ 10 min) Transfer to plate.
10. Add rest of onion mixture and tomato paste and cook over medium heat until begins to brown. Stir in processed tomatoes. Bring to simmer and cook till thickened. (~ 20 min)
11. Return meatballs and juices to skillet, reduce heat to med-low and simmer until meatballs cooked through. (~ 10 min)
12. Stir in remaining 2 tbsp basil.
