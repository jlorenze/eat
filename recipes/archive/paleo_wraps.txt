Paleo Wraps

Yield: 12

Time (hours): 45 min

Keywords: 

Recommended Sides: Chicken Salad with Bacon and Tomatoes

Ingredients:
2 cups almond flour
3/4 cup tapioca flour
1/4 cup coconut flour
2 tsp salt
1.75 cup water
2 large eggs
3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 tsp oil

Make:
1. Place 1 tsp oil in pan and heat up, using paper towel wipe skillet.
2. Pour 1/4 cup batter and tilt skillet to spread into thin layer, cook until bubbly and brown on edges. Then flip.
