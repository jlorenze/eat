BBQ Chicken Nachos

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.75

Keywords: appetizer, mexican

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
shredded cooked chicken
tortilla chips
4 roma tomatos, diced
1/2 red onion, diced
2 jalapenos, sliced
bbq sauce
8 oz Monterrey Jack cheese
8 oz Cheddar cheese
sour cream
fresh cilantro

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Toss cooked chicken in desired amount of bbq sauce.
3. Stack ingedients in two layers.
4. Bake for 15 min.
5. Garnish with plenty of cilantro and sour cream.
