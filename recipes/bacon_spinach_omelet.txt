Omelete with Bacon and Spinach

Category: Main

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: breakfast

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
8 large eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
8 slices bacon, chopped
1 onion, chopped
4 cups baby spinach

Prep:
A. onion

Make:
1. Cook bacon.
2. Beat eggs, salt, and pepper.
3. Leave 1 tbsp fat left, add onion and cook 5 min.
4. Stir in 1 cup spinach at a time and cook until wilted. (~ 1 min)
5. Add egg mixture and bacon.
