Skillet Chicken Divan

Yield: 4-6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: meat

Recommended Sides: 

Ingredients:
1 Tbsp butter
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1.25 cups water
2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 cup instant brown rice
9 oz broccoli
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Make:
1. Melt butter on med high, cook chicken 2-3 min per side, remove from pan.
2. Add water, mustard, pepper, salt, and boil.
3. Stir in rice and broccoli, bring to boil.
4. Place chicken on top, cover, cook on med-low for 10 min, or till chicken done.
5. Sprinkle cheese on top, let sit for 5 min.

Notes:
