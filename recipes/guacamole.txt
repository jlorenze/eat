Guacamole

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: mexican, appetizer

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado
onion, diced
roma tomatoes, diced
salsa
fresh cilantro, chopped
lime juice
garlic powder
salt

Make:
1. Mash avocado and add your favorite ingredients.

Notes:
1. Avocado + garlic powder + salt + lime (The Original)
2. Avocado + salsa (The Fast and Easy)
3. Avocado + onion + tomatoes + cilantro + salt + lime (The Refined)
