Black Bean Quinoa Burger

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetarian, american

Recommended Sides: roasted red potatoes, roasted asparagus, roasted cauliflower, Seasoned Potato Wedges

Ingredients:
15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1/4 cup quinoa
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup minced yellow bell
2 tbsp minced onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1.5 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp hot sauce
1 egg
3 tbsp olive oil

Make:
1. Boil quinoa and water. Reduce to simmer 15-20 min.
2. Roughly mash beans, leaving some whole beans.
3. Mix ingredients except oil, mash with hands and form patties.
4. Heat oil in skillet, cook 2-3 min per side.
