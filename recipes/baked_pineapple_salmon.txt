Baked Pineapple Salmon

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.75

Keywords: seafood

Recommended Sides: rice, coconut rice

Ingredients:
1/4 fresh pineapple, sliced into rings
2 small salmon fillets
salt
black pepper
1/2 Tbsp butter, melted
1/2 tsp Sriracha
1 tsp honey
1 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil
pinch red pepper flakes
toasted sesame seeds
green onions, thinly sliced
lime wedges

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 350F and lightly grease a baking sheet.
2. Season both sides of salmon with salt and pepper.
3. Arrange sliced pineapple on baking sheet, and place salmon on top.
4. In a small bowl, whisk butter, sriracha, honey, cilantro, garlic, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes. Brush over salmon.
5. Bake until Salmon is cooked through (~20 min). Broil for 2 minutes until salmon is lightly golden.
6. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions, serve with lime wedges.

Notes:
1. Best eaten immediately.
2. Would also be great cooked on a grill.
3. Cut remaining pineapple into chunks and freeze for smoothies or drinks!
