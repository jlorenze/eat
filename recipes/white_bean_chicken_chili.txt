White Bean Chicken Chili

Yield: 6

Time (hours):

Keywords: american, soups-stews

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
32 oz chicken broth
7 oz green chiles
1.5 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
salt
pepper
45 oz cannellini beans
8 oz cream cheese
12 oz frozen corn
3-4 cups cooked chicken
1 Tbsp lime juice
1/4 cup cilantro
shredded cheese
avocado
tortilla chips

Make:
1. Heat olive oil and saute onions (~4 min), then add garlic and saute until fragrant.
2. Add chicken broth, chiles, and spices, simmer 15 min.
3. Add beans, cream cheese, and corn, simmer for 5-10 minutes.
4. Remove some amount of soup and puree to thicken, as desired.
5. Add chicken, lime juice, and cilantro.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Top with avocado, shredded cheese, and tortilla chips, as desired.

Notes:
1. Could substitute heavy cream for cream cheese.
