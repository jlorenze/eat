Tzatziki

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sauces

Recommended Sides: pita, turkey meatballs, raw vegetables, grilled chicken

Ingredients:
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded, shredded
1 cup whole-milk Greek yogurt
2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 Tbsp minced dill and/or mint
1 clove garlic, minced
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Toss cucumber with 1/2 tsp salt and let drain for 15 min to remove excess water.
2. Whisk yogurt, oil, mint/dill, and garlic, then stir in cucumber.
3. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
