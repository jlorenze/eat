Limeade

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: drinks

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1/4 cup lime juice (4 limes)
1/4 cup sugar
water

Make:
1. Combine lemon juice and sugar and whisk until sugar dissolved.
2. Add desired amount of water, stir.
3. Optionally, chill for an hour before serving.
