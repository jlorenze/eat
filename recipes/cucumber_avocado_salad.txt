Cucumber Avocado Salad

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: vegetables, salad

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
2-3 English cucumbers, thinly sliced
1/2 pint cherry tomatoes, optional
1 avocado
salt
black pepper

Make:
1. In large bowl whisk oil, vinegar, and garlic until blended.
2. Add cucumbers, tomatoes, and avocado and toss in dressing.
3. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

Notes:
1. If making ahead of time, hold the avocado for just before serving. Best eaten within a day or two of making.
2. A little feta would be a great addition as well.
