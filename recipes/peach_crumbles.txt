Peach Crumbles

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.75

Keywords: dessert

Recommended Sides: ice cream

Ingredients:
6 ripe peaches
1 cup sugar
zest and juice of 1 lemon
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 cup AP flour
8 Tbsp butter, chilled
1/3 walnuts, chopped

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 375F.
2. Plunge peaches one by one into pot of boiling water for 2 minutes, then remove, peel, and sprinkle some drops of lemon juice on them.
3. Let peaches cool a bit and then cut into chunks. Mix with half the sugar and all of the lemon zest and cinnamon.
4. Mix remaining sugar with flour and little cubes of the chilled butter. Quickly mix with your fingers till it appears like rough breadcrumbs. If at any point it becomes too warm, pause and chill before continuing.
5. Add chopped nuts to the topping mixture.
6. Fill 4 mini cocottes with the fruit mixture and add the topping to each.
7. Bake on baking sheet to capture juices, for 20 minutes, uncovered.
8. Can be served hot or at room temperature.
