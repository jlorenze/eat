Tuscan White Bean Soup

Yield: 6-8

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: italian, soups-stews

Recommended Sides: saturday white bread

Ingredients:
1/8 cup olive oil

Make:
1. Combine 1/2 cup olive oil, lemon zest, 4 cloves sliced garlic, and sprig of rosemary in a small bowl or jar. Let sit on counter.

Notes:
1. Thanks Lauren and Miles for bringing this recipe to my attention!
2. Store leftover lemon garlic oil in refrigerator.
