Chicken Shawarma

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 1.5

Keywords: mediterranean, meat

Recommended Sides: pita, tzatziki, greek salad

Ingredients:
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
1 lb boneless skinless chicken thigh
6 Tbsp EVOO
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp allspice
3/4 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 pinch cayenne
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Slice the chicken into large chunks, approx 5-6 pieces per breast or 3-4 per thigh.
2. Whisk 4 Tbsp olive oil, spices, 3/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper. Add chicken.
3. Marinate chicken at least 1 hour or max overnight.
4. Bake at 400F on oiled baking sheet for 15 minutes or until cooked through. Turn the chicken halfway through cooking.
5. Slice the chicken into thin strips once cooled a bit.
6. Saute chicken in 2 Tbsp oil in skillet over medium heat.
