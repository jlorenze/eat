Chicken Nuggets

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: meat, american

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 medium carrot
2 cups cauliflower
1 lb ground chicken
2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp garlic powder
2/3 cup breadcrumbs

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 375F, prep tray with parchment paper.
2. Finely shred or process cauliflower and carrot.
3. Add all ingredients together, minus breadcrumbs.
4. Form ball with 1 Tbsp mixture, coat in breadcrumbs, then flatten into nugget shape.
5. Optionally spray tops with a little bit of oil.
6. Bake on parchment paper for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway.

Notes:
1. Can freeze for up to 3 months.
