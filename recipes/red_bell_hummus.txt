Roasted Red Bell Hummus

Yield: 6 to 8

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mediterranean

Recommended Sides: bread, pita, carrots, garlic hummus, eggplant roasted tomato hummus,

Ingredients:
1 red bell pepper, roasted
2 15 oz cans chickpeas, drained, liquid reserved
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cumin
1/3 cup tahini, stirred well
3 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
3/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Make:
1. Place chickpeas in food processor with garlic, salt, cumin. Process 30 seconds. Add tahini, process more.
2. Add lemon juice and 1/4 cup reserved bean liquid, process.
3. Add olive oil, process.
