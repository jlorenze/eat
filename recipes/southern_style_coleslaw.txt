Southern Style Coleslaw

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: american, salad

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp dijon mustard
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
3 Tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp onion powder
2 tsp celery seeds
16 oz coleslaw mix

Make:
1. In a large bowl mix mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt, onion powder, and celery seeds.
2. Add shredded cabbage and toss until well coated.
3. Refrigerate for an hour before serving.

Notes:
1. Best eaten within a couple days.
