Tuna Melt

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sandwich, american, seafood

Recommended Sides: tomato soup

Ingredients:
12 oz canned albacore tuna, packed in water, drained
1/3 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp finely chopped red onion
2 tsp fresh lemon juice
dried oregano
salt
pepper
bread
sliced cheese
sliced tomato, optional
slice avocado, optional

Make:
1. Mix tuna, mayonnaise, red onion, lemon juice, and spices to taste.
2. Make and toast sandwich.
3. Top with any optional ingredients.

Notes:
1. Can adjust mayo quantity as desired, or partially some plain greek yogurt.
