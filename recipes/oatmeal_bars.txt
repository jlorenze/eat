Oatmeal Bars

Yield: 9 bars

Time (hours): 0.75

Keywords: breakfast

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1.5 cups Quaker Old Fashioned Oats
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (or any nut)
1/2 cup dried fruit
1/4 cup seeds (or something else like coconut)
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1.25 cups milk
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla

Make:
1. Mix all dry, mix all wet, combine.
2. Bake 350 deg in coated 9x9 baking dish for 40 minutes.
