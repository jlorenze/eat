Whipped Cream

Yield:

Time (hours): 0.2

Keywords: dessert

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 cup cold heavy whipping cream
2 Tbsp granulated sugar
1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract

Make:
1. Whip heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla on medium-high speed until medium peaks form (~3-4 min).
2. Use immediately or cover tightly and chill in refrigerator for up to 1 day.

Notes:
1. Fun fact: "medium peaks" is a consistency somewhere in between "soft peaks" and "stiff peaks".
