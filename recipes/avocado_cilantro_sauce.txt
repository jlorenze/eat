Avocado Cilantro Sauce

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: sauces-dressings

Recommended Sides: crispy pork carnitas

Ingredients:
half an avocado
1/4 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
1/2 cup water
1 cup cilantro leaves
1 clove garlic
1/2 tsp salt
lime juice

Make:
1. Pulse all ingredients in food processor or blender until smooth. Add water to achieve desired consistency.
