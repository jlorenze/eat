Garlic Cheese Spread

Yield:

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: appetizer

Recommended Sides: bread, crackers

Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
1-2 cloves garlic, minced fine
3-4 basil leaves, minced
salt

Make:
1. Combine in small bowl, add salt to taste.
2. Add other herbs, parsley, sun-dried tomatoes, olives etc. if desired.

Notes:
Recipe from Jack Trethewey
