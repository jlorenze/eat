Sweet Potato Casserole

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: breakfast, vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
4 large sweet potatoes, chunked
1 cup walnuts, chopped
1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 tbsp cinnamon
2 tbsp olive oil

Make:
1. Boil sweet potatoes for 15-20 min until soft.
2. Preheat oven to 350F
3. Drain potatoes and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Mash while adding coconut milk.
4. Add vanilla extract and mix.
5. Move to baking dish, top with walnuts that have been tossed in remaining oil and sprinkled with cinnamon.
6. Bake for 20 min.
