Buttery Garlic Green Beans

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides: mashed potatoes

Ingredients:
1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed, halved
3 tbsp butter
3 cloves garlic, minced

Make:
1. Boil beans until soften (~5 min).
2. Drain water, add butter, cook 2-3 min.
3. Add garlic under garlic is tender and fragrant (3-4 min).
