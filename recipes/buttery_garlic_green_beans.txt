Buttery Garlic Green Beans

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides: mashed potatoes

Ingredients:
1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed, halved
3 Tbsp butter
3 cloves garlic, minced

Make:
1. Boil beans until softened (~5 min).
2. Drain water, add butter, cook 2-3 min.
3. Add garlic and cook until tender and fragrant (3-4 min).
