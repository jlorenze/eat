Apple Gorgonzola Salad

Yield: 6-8

Time (hours):

Keywords: salad

Recommended Sides: mustard vinaigrette

Ingredients:
2 romaine lettuce hearts, chopped
1 cup Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
1 green apple sliced thin
1 green apple diced
1 cup candied or toasted pecans
1/2-1 cup dried cranberries
mustard vinaigrette

Make:
1. Toss lettuce, cheese, apples, pecans, and cranberries with desired amount of mustard vinaigrette.

Notes:
1. Sub other yummy greans for romaine lettuce.
2. Candied pecans are better to balance the acidity of the dressing.
