Turkey Meatballs with Yogurt Sauce

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: mediterranean, meat, 

Recommended Sides: yogurt sauce, pita bread, cucumber avocado salad

Ingredients:
1 large egg
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 shallot, minced
handful fresh parsley, finely chopped
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/8 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 lb ground turkey
2 Tbsp olive oil

Make:
1. In mixing bowl, beat egg and add garlic, shallots, parsley, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
2. Add turkey and gently mix. Shape into 16 (~1 inch) meatballs.
3. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. 
4. Add meatballs in a single layer and cook until golden brown (~2 min). Flip and repeat, until cooked through.
5. Serve with yogurt sauce.

Notes:
1. Would also be good with ground lamb, or mixture of lamb and turkey.
2. Freeze uncooked, shaped meatballs or cooked meatballs for up to 3 months.
3. To prepare frozen, cooked meatballs, defrost and then gently reheat, covered, in a 350F oven until warmed through.
