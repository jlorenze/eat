Sushi Rice

Yield: 8

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: asian

Recommended Sides: Sushi (Spicy Tuna Roll), Sushi (California Roll), Sushi (Vegetarian Roll), Sushi (Philly Roll)

Ingredients:
2 cups white rice (sushi rice)
1/2 cup rice vinegar
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1/4 cup white sugar
1 tsp salt

Make:
1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Cook via package instructions.
2. Combine vinegar, oil, sugar, salt, heat for sugar to dissolve.
3. Stir into cooked rice once cooled slightly.
