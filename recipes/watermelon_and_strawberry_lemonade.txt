Watermelon and Strawberry Lemonade

Yield: 2

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: drinks

Recommended Sides: warm summer day, friends

Ingredients:
2 cups watermelon, cubed
1/4 cup strawberries, halved
3 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup water

Make:
1. Combine ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.

Notes:
1. Can you use frozen watermelon?
