Broccoli Salad

Yield: 10

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: salad

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
broccoli, chopped
1 cup shredded colby cheese
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
6 strips bacon, crumbled
red onion
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 Tbsp vinegar
1 cup mayonnaise

Make:
1. Mix together mayonnaise, sugar, and vinegar.
2. Mix everything together.

Notes:
1. A classic Thanksgiving dish, also great for cookouts.
