Ramen

Yield: 2-3

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: asian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 packs instant ramen noodles
1 Tbsp sesame oil
1 Tbsp ghee
3 scallions, thinly sliced
2-4 tsp chili sauce
1 lb firm tofu, pressed
4 cups broth, vegetable or chicken
3 Tbsp mirin
3 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
2 Tbsp miso paste
spinich (optional)
kimchi (optional)
soft boiled egg (optional)

Make:
1. Bring water to rapid boil in dutch oven and cook noodles until just tender (~2 min). Then drain in colander and toss with sesame oil.
2. Heat ghee in dutch oven over medium heat and add scallion whites, chili sauce, and tofu and cook for 4-5 minutes to give tofu a little color.
3. Add broth and bring to simmer. Add mirin, soy, and miso and simmer for about 4 minutes until miso is dissolved.
4. Remove from heat and mix in noodles, and other desired toppings.

Notes:
1. Can subsitute 1/2 lb ground pork for tofu.
2. Don't add seasoning packet from ramen noodles.

Cite:
Fahr, Yasmin. Keeping It Simple.
