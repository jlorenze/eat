Yogurt Sauce

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: sauces

Recommended Sides: turkey meatballs with yogurt sauce, pita bread

Ingredients:
1/2 cup regular or Greek yogurt
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 Tbsp white wine vinegar
1 Tbsp chopped fresh dill
salt
black pepper

Make:
1. Mash garlic into a paste with a pinch of salt.
2. Mix all ingredients, adding a little water to thin as needed.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
