Caprese Salad

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: italian, salad

Recommended Sides: pasta carbonara, spaghetti with arugula and proscuitto

Ingredients:
2 large ripe and meaty tomatos
8 oz fresh mozzarella
fresh basil
olive oil
balsamic vinegar
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella and arrange, topped with basil.
2. Season with salt and pepper, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar.
