Halibut with Coconut-Curry Broth

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: seafood

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
2 tsp canola oil
2 large shallots, finely chopped
2 tsp red curry paste
2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1/2 cup unsweetened light coconut milk
3/4 tsp salt
4 5-oz halibut fillets
5 oz baby spinach leaves
2 cups cooked brown rice
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, coarsely chopped
2 scallions, thinly sliced
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
black pepper

Make:
1. Heat oil over medium heat. Add shallots and cook until beginning to brown (~4 min). Add curry paste and cook until fragrant.
2. Add broth, coconut milk, and 1/2 tsp salt and simmer until reduced to 2 cups (~5 min).
3. Season halibut with 1/4 tsp salt, add to pan and coat with sauce. Cover and cook until fish flakes easily (~7 min).
4. Microwave spinach in bowl with tight lid for 2 min.
5. Add rice and spinach to bowl, top with fish.
6. Add cilantro, scallions, lime juice, and some pepper to the sauce to season, then ladle over the fish.
