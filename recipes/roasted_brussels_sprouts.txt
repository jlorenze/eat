Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 lb brussels sprouts
olive oil
salt
pepper
red pepper flakes (optional)
balsamic vinegar
honey

Make:
1. Preheat oven to 425F.
2. Slice sprouts in half and toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
3. Roast 20-30 minutes or until tender.
4. Toss in mixture of balsamic vinegar and honey to taste.
