Green Pea Soup

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 0.25

Keywords: vegetables, soups-stews

Recommended Sides: sandwiches

Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped roughly
2.5 cups chicken broth
black pepper
10 oz frozen peas

Make:
1. Heat oil over medium heat and cook onion until soft.
2. Add broth and some black pepper, bring to boil.
3. Add peas and cook just until heated through.
4. Puree soup using blender.

Notes:
1. Can serve hot or cold.
