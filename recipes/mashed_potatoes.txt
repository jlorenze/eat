Creamy Mashed Yukon Gold Potatoes

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 0.5

Keywords: vegetables

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1.75 - 2 lbs yukon gold potatoes, cut into large chunks
2 cloves garlic
salt
pepper
2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
1 cup milk, heated

Make:
1. Put potatoes in pot and cover with cold water by an inch.
2. Add 1/2 tsp salt and boil.
3. Simmer 15-20 minutes till tender.
4. Drain, dump back into pot, mash.
5. Beat in butter and hot milk by 1/4 cup increments.
6. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic if desired.
