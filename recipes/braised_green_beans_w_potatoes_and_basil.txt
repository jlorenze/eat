Braised Green Beans with Potatoes and Basil

Yield: 6

Time (hours): 1

Keywords: mediterranean, soups-stews, vegetarian

Recommended Sides: fresh bread

Ingredients:
5 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped fine
2 tsp dried oregano
4 garlic cloves, minced
1.5 cups water
1.5 lbs greenbeans, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
1 lb Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 tsp baking soda
15 oz can diced tomatoes, drained with juice reserved, chopped
1 Tbsp tomato paste
salt
pepper
3 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
lemon juice

Make:
1. Heat 3 Tbsp oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened (~5 min). Stir in oregano and garlic and cook until fragrant.
2. Stir in water, green beans, potatoes, and baking soda. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 min, stirring occasionally.
3. Stir in tomatoes and their juice, tomato paste, 2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper. Cover and simmer until sauce has thickened and green beans can be easily cut (~40 min).
4. Stir in basil and season to taste with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
5. Serve drizzled with remaining 2 Tbsp of olive oil.
