Teriyaki Stir Fry

Yield: 4

Time (hours): 1
 
Keywords: asian

Recommended Sides:

Ingredients:
1 cup dry rice
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 tsp garlic powder
pepper
3 Tbsp cornstarch
3 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion
4 cups broccoli florets
red bell pepper
2 carrots
green beans
1 cup chicken broth
6 Tbsp mirin
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tsp rice vinegar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp garlic powder

Make:
1. Cook rice.
2. Chop all veggies to bite sizes for even cooking.
3. Whisk together broth, mirin, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, 2 Tbsp cornstarch, ginger, 1 tsp garlic powder.
4. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces, sprinkle with 1/2 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, and 3 Tbsp cornstarch.
5. Heat 2 Tbsp olive oil over med-high heat, cook chicken pieces 3-4 minutes per side to brown and cook through.
6. Transfer cooked chicken to a plate.
7. Add remaining 1 Tbsp oil to hot pan, cook veggies to be tender-crisp.
8. Add chicken back in and add sauce mixture. Simmer until thickened (~1 min).
