Mustard Vinaigrette

Yield: 1 cup

Time (hours): 0.1

Keywords: sauces-dressings

Recommended Sides: apple gorgonzola salad

Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
1/4 red onion
2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp oregano
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
salt
pepper

Make:
1. Blend garlic, red onion, mustrd, vinegar, oil until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
